# Mystic Bot Privacy Policy **Effective Date:** July 23, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains what information Mystic Bot collects and how it is used. ## 1. Information We Collect Depending on enabled features, Mystic Bot may store: - Discord User IDs - Discord Server (Guild) IDs - Channel IDs - Role IDs - Command usage information - Server configuration - Ticket information - Moderation logs - Giveaway information Mystic Bot does not collect passwords or Discord tokens. ## 2. Why We Collect Data Collected data is used to: - Provide bot functionality. - Save server settings. - Process commands. - Operate ticket systems. - Manage giveaways. - Store moderation records. - Improve reliability and performance. ## 3. Data Storage Data is stored securely and only retained as long as necessary to provide the Bot's features. ## 4. Data Sharing We do not sell personal information. Information is only shared when: - Required by law. - Necessary to protect the Bot or its users. - Required to provide the Bot's functionality. ## 5. Data Deletion Server owners may request deletion of their server's stored data by contacting the Bot developers. Some data may be automatically removed when the Bot leaves a server. ## 6. Security Reasonable measures are taken to protect stored information. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. ## 7. Third-Party Services Mystic Bot operates on Discord and may rely on hosting providers or database services. Their privacy policies also apply. ## 8.
